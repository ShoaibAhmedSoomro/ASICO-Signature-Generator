## ğŸš€ Quick Start

### Option 1: Use the Generator (Recommended)

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Fill in your details:
   - **Full Name** (English & Arabic)
   - **Job Title** (English & Arabic)
   - **Email Address**
3. Click **"Generate Signature"**
4. Click **"Copy HTML"** or **"Download"**
5. Paste into your email client's signature settings

### Option 2: Manual Template

1. Click **"Get Template Manually"** button in the generator
2. Or open `template.html` directly
3. Replace placeholder text with your information
4. Copy the HTML and paste into your email client
